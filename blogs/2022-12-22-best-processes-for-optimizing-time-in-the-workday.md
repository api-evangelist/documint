How Do You Get Started Optimizing Your Workday? It's important to start small. Break up your workday into manageable chunks by beginning with the essential tasks. When planning, it's necessary to consider your goals and expectations. Make a routine and try to keep yourself motivated throughout the day. By staying focused and motivated, you will accomplish more in less time and feel more productive overall.
